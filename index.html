About Us

Local powder‑coating shop focused on quality prep and consistent curing. Every part is degreased, media‑blasted, and inspected before coating so your finish looks great and lasts longer.

  • Professional media blasting & prep
  • Thousands of colors and textures
  • Quick quotes & realistic lead times

Why Powder Coat?

Powder coating is tougher than liquid paint, highly chip‑ and corrosion‑resistant, and eco‑friendly. Perfect for parts that need to look good and work hard.
